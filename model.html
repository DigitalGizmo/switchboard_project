Switchboard Project

You're the Operator!

Your Job

To pick up a call:
Drag the plug into the socket with the red flashing light.

To connect a call:
Drag the other end of the cable into the requested socket.

Key

  • red flashing light indicates an incoming call
  • green steady light indicates a line is busy on a call
